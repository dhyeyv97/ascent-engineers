About Us

about us

Your Trusted Partner in Construction Excellence

Ascent Engineers and Contractors is a well-established name in the construction industry, offering a wide range of services including Design, Construction Management, Piling, Rock Anchoring, Geotechnical Investigation, and Digital Survey Activities. With over 25 successful projects completed in the past 10 years, the company has built a solid reputation for excellence and reliability. The firm’s success is attributed to its innovative and practical approach, with a strong emphasis on adapting to site conditions, leveraging technical expertise, and utilizing a skilled and talented team.

Founded in 2012, Ascent Engineers and Contractors initially specialized in Digital Survey services. However, by 2017, the company expanded its portfolio to include Construction, Piling, Geotechnical Investigation, and other specialized services. Today, Ascent Engineers and Contractors has grown to handle a diverse range of projects, including complex structures, high-rise towers, and industrial buildings. Despite its extensive portfolio, the firm continues to provide a fresh, tailored approach to every new project, ensuring that each assignment is handled with utmost dedication and professionalism.

At Ascent Engineers and Contractors, the company’s values are rooted in a strong commitment to quality and innovation. The leadership team fosters an environment that encourages growth, both professionally and personally, for every individual involved in the company. Their people-oriented approach not only helps in achieving success but also builds strong relationships with clients, ensuring sustainable growth for the firm.
